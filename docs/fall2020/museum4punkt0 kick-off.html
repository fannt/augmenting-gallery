museum4punkt0 kick-off

augmenting-the-gallery

overview

This week, we have our first meeting with the M4P0 team. After learning about their use cases and their visions, we will assemble into groups to work on each of those for the rest of the semester.

Once the groups are assigned, the rest of the session will be dedicated to finishing up the Augmented Exercises in Unity, as well as clarifying any issues that remain with Unity development. It's important to get these out of the way before we move on to more conceptual approaches on the topic of museums and digital media.

m4p0

project

the Museum4Punkt0 initiative is a federal program aimed at modernizing museums across Germany.

the final presentation of your proposal and prototype will be on december 10 (last day of classes), to the whole M4P0 team.

people

  • alan prohm, digital concept and coordination for the humboldt forum
  • cristina navarro, digital concept and coordination for the staatliche museen
  • ceren topcu, communication and concept for gemäldegalerie

use-cases

guidelines

your final presentation should include:

  • a writeup of your concept proposal
    • what you're proposing
    • why you think it's important
    • how you propose to implement it
  • a prototype using AR
    • demonstrating the core features of the app
    • demonstrating the look and feel of your app
